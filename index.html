About me

Welcome! I am Zhonghao Lyu. I am now a Postdoc Research Fellow at KTH Royal Institute of Technology. I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer & Information Engineering from The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen. Before that, I received my M.Phil. degree from Information & Communication Engineering, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2021, and the Bachelor degree in Electronic Information Engineering from Dalian University of Technology (DUT) in 2018. My research interests include Edge AI, Large AI Models, and UAV Communications.
