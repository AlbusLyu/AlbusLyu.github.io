About me

Hi! My name is Yuchen Li. I am a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate in Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, where I am advised by Prof. Linghe Kong and Prof. Guihai Chen. Since September 2021, I have been doing my internship in Big Data Laboratory at Baidu Inc., where I am mentored by Dr. Haoyi Xiong.
Research interests:
My research mainly focuses on information retrieval, recommender systems and data mining. In particular, I am interested in the application of learing to rank & pre-training for large-scale web search, large lanugage models and recommender systems.
